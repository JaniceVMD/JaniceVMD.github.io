Adobe Animate

Using my illustrator final project, I used Adobe Animate to bring the project to life with animation. I had this grand idea in mind but with my limited skills in Animate, I wasn't able to fully accomplish my original idea. Nevertheless, I am still happy with how the animation turned out. 

In the beginning of the animation, I animated different silhouettes of pets that I created in Photoshop. Then, I introduced the pink parrot coming towards the viewer on the tree branch. To finish it off, I animated the text, CJ’s Pet Store. To enhance the animation, I also added a tropical rainforest sound effect in the background.
